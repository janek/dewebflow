WTFH: TRUST

as in legal arrangement.

as in belief.

as in organization.

as in confidence.

These 5 letters stand for many things. The event TRUST wants to discuss ways, possibilities, and strategies of systems we trust, in which we feel safe, systems that challenge us or that are new, and systems that work. The term TRUST popped up several times during the panels and discussions of the last event. We realize this next event to shine a light on the trust in oneself, the trust in collaboration, the trust in collectives, and the trust in the different communities within the club and performance culture to find out what the future holds.

WTFH is dedicated to queer & feminist club and creative culture.

WTFH aims to emphasize, network and promote the work of FLINTA* artists.

WTFH sets the working environments, individual & collective strategies as well as the experiences of and by FLINTA* artists to focus.

We believe everyone should have access to equal opportunities, treatment and pay.
