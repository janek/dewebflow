WTFH: ENOUGH

Enough. Done. Basta.

Enough. Sufficient. Ample.

The first WTFH event of 2022 takes this condition as a starting point to look at the past, current and future dynamics of a broader Berlin club and creative scene.

We invite you to join us for our initial Conference Day, hosted by a multitude of practitioners and visionaries we admire for their different initiatives to shape a more equal, equitable and fun scene in Berlin.

Reflecting these shifts to a new future, the artists at our subsequent Club night stand for a genre-bending take on sonic euphoria, breaking with Berlin's patriarchal four-to-the-floor obsession in celebration of transcendence through diversity.Join our queer and feminist Conference Day and Club Night @ Alte Mnze Berlin.
